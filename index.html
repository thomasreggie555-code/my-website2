Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most.

Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password." Enter your registered email address and we will send a secure reset link within 2 minutes. If you do not receive it, check your spam folder or contact support via live chat.
Bitcoin transactions require network confirmations before they complete. Depending on current blockchain congestion and the transaction fee set, this can take anywhere from a few minutes to several hours. ZK Bank requires a minimum of 3 confirmations for BTC deposits. You can track progress using your transaction ID on any public blockchain explorer.
Log into your ZK Bank account, navigate to the transaction in question, and click "Report Issue." Alternatively, contact our 24/7 fraud hotline immediately. We will place a hold on the transaction and begin a formal investigation within 1 business day.
Standard accounts have a daily transfer limit of $25,000 for domestic and $10,000 for international transfers. Premium account holders have higher limits. To request a temporary or permanent limit increase, contact your dedicated relationship manager or submit a request through the app.
International SWIFT transfers typically arrive within 1 to 5 business days, depending on the destination country, intermediary banks, and any local compliance requirements. Transfers to select countries with a ZK Bank partner network arrive within 24 hours.
Yes. ZK Bank supports external crypto wallet withdrawals for Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and USDT. Go to your Crypto Wallet section, select the asset, enter the destination wallet address, double-check the network type, and confirm with your security pin. Ensure the address and network match to avoid permanent loss of funds.
Open the ZK Bank app, go to Settings, then Security. Tap "Enable Two-Factor Authentication" and choose your preferred method: SMS code or an authenticator app. We recommend using an authenticator app like Google Authenticator for stronger protection.
